Welcome!

Cooking is not only about nourishing our bodies; it is so much more.

  • Through cooking we express our creativity.
  • Through cooking we show our love for those around us.
  • Through cooking we build community and continue our family traditions.

It is through cooking that we explore an integral part of other cultures, and in doing so create delicious dishes to please the palatte.

FOOD AS CULTURE

Using this website we hope that you will explore the dishes of cultures from around this planet.

Some you will love and perhaps bring into your family as a new rotating dish.

Some will fail to thrill and be forgotten.

Some will turn out to be completely unappealing to your palatte.

But all will be successes in the mission to introduce you to new culinary viewpoints!

Regardless of each dish's outcome with you and yours, we hope you will continue on in your exploration of the dishes made by your fellow humans across the globe.

-The Joys of Cooking team
